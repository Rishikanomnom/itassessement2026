Ace School (2015-2022)

My early academic journey started here. I studied from grade 3 to 10 in this school. This school hand left such an impact on me that i believe that this is where my actual education started. It will always be like a family to me as the school grew with me.

We used to always have something called the star of the month. For any student who did a good job, teachers would provide them with smileys and once calculated would be awarded to the one with the highest number. I was a daily visitor!

My academic integrity and honesty started from Ace. As they gave one on one attention to every student. This made me blossom from an average student to an amazing one in matter of 2 to 3 years.

The Annual day tradition is something that used to be carried out to showcase any type of dance, music or drama to the parents present. Most of my fond memories were from then. I took dance as well as music as a hobby due to it. The picture shown here is for the annual day when i was in Grade 4.

The Sports day was one of my most hatest event i eould ever take part on, as I was just not good enough. I did play chess which intriguied me but running wasn't my best speciality.

Lastly the graduation felt more shocking than sad. When i looked back into what i had gone through as well as the up and downs and the achievements i have garnered, it seemed like an incredible journey a fever dream to come out of.
